# EcoHabit - Product Context

## Product Concept: Self-Happiness Driven Recycling

### Core Philosophy
Transform recycling from external obligation to internal satisfaction through psychological reward systems that make environmental action feel personally rewarding and meaningful.

### Behavioral Psychology Principles

#### 1. Instant Feedback & Micro-Rewards
- Visual rewards: sparkles, confetti, animations after every action
- Positive affirmations: "You just saved 0.5 kg of plastic – amazing!"
- Dopamine triggers similar to mobile games

#### 2. Progress Visualization
- Streaks & Levels: "You've recycled 10 items this week → Level 2: Plastic Warrior"
- Cumulative impact: "Your efforts have saved X liters of water / X kg of plastic"
- Tangible effect visualization of small actions

#### 3. Narrative & Storytelling
- Personal mission stories: "Your neighborhood was drowning in plastic. You collected 20 bottles this week!"
- Mini-stories make experience immersive → user feels like a hero
- Emotional connection to environmental impact

#### 4. Social Proof & Community
- Leaderboards for friendly competition
- Shareable achievements for social validation
- Group challenges create habit loops through peer pressure

#### 5. Gamified Content Flow
- Daily Eco Quest: 1-2 min micro-actions with positive reinforcement
- Mini-games/quizzes: drag and drop sorting, knowledge tests
- Unlockables: badges, animated trophies, avatar customization

#### 6. Psychological Hooks
- Variable rewards: sometimes points, sometimes badges, sometimes stories
- Surprise & delight: random recognition for actions
- Immediate reflection: show impact after every task

## User Journey & MVP Flow

### 1. Onboarding Experience
- Welcome screen: "Turn Recycling into a Fun Daily Habit"
- Quick tutorial: explain points, streaks, badges, mini-games
- Goal setting: personal recycling targets (e.g., "Recycle 10 items per week")
- Waste category preferences: Plastic, Paper, E-Waste, Organic
- Avatar selection: personal eco-hero identity

### 2. Daily Dashboard
- Daily Challenge prominently displayed
- Quick Log Action button ("I recycled today")
- Streak tracker with fire emoji
- Progress stats: trees saved, plastic avoided
- Emotional feedback messages

### 3. Action Logging (Self-Reported)
- Select waste type from categories
- Input quantity with validation
- Optional photo upload for social sharing
- Instant points calculation and display
- Impact message: "You saved 2 turtles today!"

### 4. Educational Content (25-Day Program)
- **Day 1**: Awareness - The Hidden Plastic Problem
- **Day 2**: Empowerment - Small Actions Matter  
- **Day 3**: Knowledge - Sorting Waste Correctly
- **Day 4**: Empathy - Impact on Wildlife
- **Day 5**: E-Waste & Toxicity
- **Day 6**: Local Impact & Community
- **Day 7**: Reflection & Habit Formation
- **Days 8-25**: Advanced topics, Sri Lankan context, sustainability practices

### 5. Gamification Mechanics

#### Points System
- Plastic: 10 points/item
- Paper: 5 points/item  
- E-waste: 20 points/item
- Organic: 8 points/item
- Bonus multipliers for larger quantities

#### Streak System
- Daily logging maintains streak
- Streak milestones: 3, 7, 14, 30, 100 days
- Streak recovery: one missed day per week allowed
- Streak freeze: emergency protection

#### Badge Collection
- **Eco Starter**: First action logged
- **Plastic Protector**: 10 plastic items recycled
- **Streak Warrior**: 7-day streak maintained
- **Eco Community Builder**: Invite 3 friends
- **Eco Hero Level 1**: Complete 7-day lesson program

#### Level Progression
- Progressive level system: 100, 300, 600, 1000, 1500 points
- Level titles: Eco Beginner → Plastic Warrior → Green Guardian
- Unlock new features and content at higher levels

### 6. AI Chat & Reflection
- Gemini-powered eco-coach for personalized guidance
- Daily reflection prompts: "How do you feel about today's action?"
- Encouragement and motivation based on user progress
- Educational Q&A about environmental topics

### 7. Community Features
- Leaderboards: Global, Friends, Local
- Social sharing: Achievement cards for social media
- Friend connections: Invite and compete with friends
- Community challenges: Group goals and competitions

### 8. Recycler Locator
- Google Maps integration showing nearby recycling centers
- Filter by waste type accepted
- Operating hours and contact information
- Directions integration with native maps

## Sri Lankan Market Context

### Localization Requirements
- **Languages**: English, Sinhala (සිංහල), Tamil (தமிழ்)
- **Cultural Sensitivity**: Respectful messaging, local environmental issues
- **Regional Challenges**: Coastal pollution, dengue prevention, water conservation
- **Local Recyclers**: Integration with 16 licensed e-waste collectors

### Market-Specific Features
- **Dengue Prevention**: Connect waste management to health outcomes
- **Tourism Impact**: Environmental action for tourist areas
- **Agricultural Context**: Composting and organic waste management
- **Religious Considerations**: Respectful timing and messaging

### Waste Categories (Sri Lankan Context)
- Organic/Biodegradable (60% of waste)
- Plastic (12% - major concern)
- Paper & Cardboard (12%)
- E-waste (1.5% - growing concern)
- Glass (3%)
- Metal (2%)

## Key Features Summary

### Core Features
1. **Habit Tracking**: Daily logging with streak maintenance
2. **Gamification**: Points, levels, badges, leaderboards
3. **Educational Content**: 25-day lesson program with stories
4. **AI Chat**: Personalized eco-coaching and reflection
5. **Community**: Social features and group challenges
6. **Maps**: Recycler locator with directions

### Psychological Design
- **Emotion First**: Stories and visuals before facts
- **Micro-Actions**: Small, achievable daily tasks
- **Immediate Rewards**: Instant gratification for actions
- **Social Validation**: Peer recognition and competition
- **Progress Visualization**: Clear impact measurement
- **Habit Reinforcement**: Consistent positive feedback loops
